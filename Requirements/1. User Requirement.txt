These requirements focus on what the end-users expect from the platform.

    Users must be able to:
        Register, log in, and manage their accounts securely.
        Browse, search, and purchase e-books easily.
        View personalized recommendations for books.
        Manage their shopping cart, wishlist, and track orders.
        Make secure payments using various gateways like Stripe or PayPal.
        Interact with an AI-powered chatbot for support and book suggestions.
        Receive notifications via email/SMS about order status, discounts, and new arrivals.
        Choose between light and dark modes for better user experience.
        Access the platform from multiple devices with a responsive design.
        Experience multilingual support for global accessibility.
